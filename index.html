Location Map

If Progress Had An Address Turbhe, Navi Mumbai

Turbhe MIDC, the premiere business district of Navi Mumbai is a dynamic address at the very heart of Navi Mumbai, flanked by prime locales Vashi and Nerul on either sides, Turbhe is poised as an prima facie investment destination geared for progress. Turbhe harnesses the MIDC Industrial belt, the APMC Market zone and encompasses the exclusive attributes of super fast connectivity through Rail, Road and Metro. On the very brink of the Navi Mumbai Intl. Airport, Atul Setu, CIDCO Gold Line Metro and the Mumbai Pune Highway, the location has arrived as a to make its mark on the Navi Mumbai realty landscape, and the best is yet to come.
